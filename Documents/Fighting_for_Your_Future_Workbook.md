## The Decision Stack

Here's the truth: Your future isn't built on one big decision. It's built on a thousand small ones.

# RESOURCES

## When You Need Help

If you're in crisis or need someone to talk to:

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text **988** (free, 24/7)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text **HOME** to **741741**
- **National Child Abuse Hotline:** 1-800-422-4453
- **National Sexual Assault Hotline:** 1-800-656-4673

## If You're in Trouble with the Law

- **Public Defenders' Offices** — everyone is entitled to a lawyer, use it
- **Legal Aid** — free legal help for qualifying individuals
- **Your School Counselor** — can connect you to resources

## If You Need Mentorship

- **Big Brothers Big Sisters:** www.bbbs.org
- **Boys & Girls Clubs:** www.bgca.org
- **4-H:** www.4-h.org
