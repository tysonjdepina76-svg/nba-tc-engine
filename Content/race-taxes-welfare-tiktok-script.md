## 1. WHO LIVES IN THE U.S.? (US Census Bureau)

- White: ~61.6%
- Hispanic/Latino: ~19.1%
- Black/African American: ~13.6%
- Asian: ~6.3%

---

## 2. WHO PAYS THE TOTAL TAX MOUNTAIN? (Gross Dollars)

- 🥇 White Americans pay the highest TOTAL dollar amount into the U.S. treasury (Over 70% of total tax revenue).
- Why? Because they hold over 80% of the country's generational wealth and occupy the highest-income brackets.

---

## 3. WHO FACES THE HIGHEST TAX BURDEN? (The W-2 Trap)

- 💸 Black and Hispanic middle/working-class workers pay the highest percentage of their income from their actual paychecks.
- Why? They rely almost entirely on W-2 wage income. W-2 money is automatically taxed at the absolute highest, unescapable rates.
- Wealthy individuals (who are disproportionately white) make their money off stocks and investments, which are taxed at much lower "Capital Gains" rates.

---

## 4. WHO USES THE MOST WELFARE? (Total Dollars)

- 🥇 White Americans are numerically the #1 recipients of welfare, food stamps (SNAP), and Medicaid.
- Why? Because they are the majority of the population. There are more white people on government benefits than any other race.

---

## THE HISTORY FACT CHECK

The U.S. welfare state was created by white politicians under the 1935 Social Security Act. It explicitly excluded agricultural and domestic workers—which legally locked out 60% of Black workers from day one. Black families couldn't get equal legal access to welfare until the late 1960s after Civil Rights lawsuits forced the system to open up.
