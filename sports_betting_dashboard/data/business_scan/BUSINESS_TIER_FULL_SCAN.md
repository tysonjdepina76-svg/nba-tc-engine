## 5. PINNACLE-ANCHORED EDGES (Sports with Pinnacle available)

### Pinnacle available on: MLB, World Cup, Tennis

These 3 sports can produce **vig-removed true probabilities** anchored against Pinnacle's sharp pricing, with **auto-flagged mispricings** on any book that deviates from the Pinnacle-implied fair line.

**How it works:**
1. Take Pinnacle outcomes → convert American odds to implied probabilities
2. Strip the vig (normalize to 100%)
3. Convert any other book's odds to implied probabilities
4. Edge = (book_decimal − fair_decimal) × 100

**Sample live edges detected NOW:**
