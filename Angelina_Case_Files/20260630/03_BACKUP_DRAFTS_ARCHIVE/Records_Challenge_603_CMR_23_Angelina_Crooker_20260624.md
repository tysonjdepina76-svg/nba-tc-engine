## I. PURPOSE

Pursuant to 603 CMR 23.10, I hereby challenge the accuracy, completeness, and procedural validity of records maintained by Wareham High School concerning Angelina Karima Crooker. This challenge is filed within the timelines established by 603 CMR 23.00 and seeks correction or removal of inaccurate, misleading, or procedurally invalid information in the student record.

## II. RECORDS CHALLENGED

The undersigned challenges the following entries in the student record of Angelina Karima Crooker:

1. **The school's claim of 30 absences**, which is contradicted by the PowerSchool record of 16 days. The 14-day discrepancy is unexplained. The 30-day claim is unsupported by the authoritative attendance record.

2. **The summer school requirement** imposed on June 23, 2026, which is procedurally invalid because:
   - No pre-penalty intervention steps under Page 44 of the Wareham High School Handbook were taken.
   - No M.G.L. c. 71, § 37H½ hearing was held.
   - No M.G.L. c. 71, § 37H written notice was provided.
   - No IEP team review was convened under 34 CFR § 300.324.
   - No manifestation determination review was conducted under 34 CFR § 300.530.

3. **The NC/CP designations** placed on the transcript, which are procedurally invalid for the same reasons stated in (2) above.

4. **Any record entries** that describe the absences as "unexcused" without addressing the disability-related context of which the school was on notice (bullying incidents, late-day concentration of absences).

5. **Any record entries** that refer to a "habitual truancy" determination, which is contradicted by M.G.L. c. 119, § 21 (her highest quarter was five unexcused, not eight).

6. **Any record entries** that refer to a "chronic absentee" determination, which is contradicted by the DESE standard (18 days; she had 16).

## III. RELIEF REQUESTED

Pursuant to 603 CMR 23.10(2), the undersigned requests that the principal:

2. **Correct or remove** each of the challenged entries described in Section II.

3. **Provide the parent with a written decision** stating whether the challenged entry will be corrected, removed, or retained, and the basis for that decision.

4. **Provide the parent with notice of the right to a superintendent review** under 603 CMR 23.10(3) of any decision to retain the challenged entry.

5. **Provide the parent with notice of the right to file a complaint** with the Department of Elementary and Secondary Education under 603 CMR 23.10(4).
