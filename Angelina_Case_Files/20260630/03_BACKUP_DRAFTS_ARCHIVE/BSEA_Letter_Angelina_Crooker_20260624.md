## IV. CASE LAW

The procedural protections of M.G.L. c. 71, § 37H½ apply to any sanction that operates as a serious disciplinary consequence, including permanent transcript designations. *See* *Goodwin v. Lee*, 480 Mass. 79 (2018); *Hampden 2017, LLC v. Boston*, No. SUCV2017-02159 (Mass. Sup. Ct. 2017). A change in placement under the IDEA includes any removal in excess of ten school days, and requires a manifestation determination review before the change takes effect. 34 CFR §§ 300.530, 300.536.
