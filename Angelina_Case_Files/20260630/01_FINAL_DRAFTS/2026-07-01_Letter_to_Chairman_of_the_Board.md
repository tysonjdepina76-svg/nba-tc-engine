# Letter to the Chairman of the School Committee

**Date:** July 1, 2026
**From:** Sarah Crooker, parent
**To:** Chairman, Wareham School Committee
**Re:** Request for Emergency Fair Hearing — Angelina Crooker

---

Dear Chairman,

I am writing to request your immediate assistance in scheduling an emergency Fair Hearing before the School Committee regarding the actions of Wareham High School that have affected my daughter, Angelina Crooker.

## Urgency

This matter is urgent because:

1. **Summer programming has already begun** — Angelina is being denied access to credit recovery and summer school programming that is currently in session.

2. **Final exam decisions are final** — The school has declared its decisions "final and not subject to further review," yet these decisions will have lasting academic consequences for Angelina.

3. **The 2026-2027 school year is approaching** — Without an IEP Team meeting, Angelina will enter the new school year without appropriate accommodations or modifications.

4. **The principal is unavailable** — Principal Palladino is out of the office from June 26 through July 7, 2026, which is the critical window for engagement.

5. **Records request is pending** — The school has not responded to my records request, which is preventing me from preparing a complete case.

## Background

Angelina Crooker is a Grade 9 student at Wareham High School with a documented disability. She has an active IEP. Beginning in late May 2026, she experienced an acute medical condition that resulted in medically necessary absences. All absences were documented by her physician and communicated to the school in advance.

Despite this, the school has taken multiple adverse actions against Angelina without following required procedures. The school has:

- Denied Angelina the opportunity to take final exams
- Denied Angelina enrollment in summer credit recovery
- Denied Angelina participation in summer school programming
- Refused to convene an IEP Team meeting
- Refused to consider whether absences were disability-related
- Failed to provide prior written notice
- Failed to obtain parental consent

## Request for Emergency Hearing

I am requesting that the School Committee convene an emergency Fair Hearing as soon as possible. I am available to meet at any time that is convenient for the Committee.

## Specific Issues

I am requesting that the Fair Hearing address:

1. **Reversal of the school's decisions** denying Angelina educational opportunities.

2. **Convening of an IEP Team meeting** before the start of the 2026-2027 school year.

3. **Reclassification of all 18 absences** as medically excused and disability-related.

4. **Provision of compensatory education** for the educational opportunities Angelina has been denied.

5. **Implementation of systemic changes** to prevent similar violations in the future.

I am committed to working with the School Committee to resolve this matter in a way that protects Angelina's rights. I trust that you will give this matter your immediate attention.
