# Letter to the School Committee

**Date:** July 1, 2026
**From:** Sarah Crooker, parent
**To:** Wareham School Committee
**Re:** Request for Fair Hearing — Angelina Crooker

---

Dear Members of the School Committee,

I am writing to formally request a Fair Hearing before the Wareham School Committee regarding the actions of Wareham High School that have affected my daughter, Angelina Crooker.

## Basis for the Request

Pursuant to MGL c. 71, § 37H 1/2 and the district's established procedures, parents have the right to request a Fair Hearing before the School Committee to challenge decisions made by school administration.

I am requesting such a hearing regarding the following decisions:

1. The decision to deny Angelina the opportunity to take final exams
2. The decision to deny Angelina enrollment in summer credit recovery
3. The decision to deny Angelina participation in summer school programming
4. The refusal to convene an IEP Team meeting
5. The refusal to consider whether absences were disability-related

## Background

Angelina Crooker is a Grade 9 student with a documented disability. She has an active IEP. Beginning in late May 2026, Angelina experienced an acute medical condition that required ongoing treatment. All absences in June 2026 were medically documented and communicated to the school in advance.

Despite this, the school has taken multiple adverse actions against Angelina without following the procedural requirements of IDEA, 603 CMR 28.00, and MGL c. 71, § 37H 1/2. Specifically, the school has:

- Denied educational opportunities to a student with a disability
- Failed to convene the IEP Team despite multiple parent requests
- Failed to provide prior written notice
- Failed to obtain parental consent
- Failed to consider whether absences were disability-related
- Failed to provide procedural safeguards

## Issues to Be Addressed

I am requesting that the Fair Hearing address the following issues:

1. **Whether the school's actions were consistent with IDEA, 603 CMR 28.00, and MGL c. 71, § 37H 1/2.**

2. **Whether the school is required to convene an IEP Team meeting** to review all decisions affecting Angelina.

3. **Whether the absences should be reclassified** as medically excused and disability-related.

4. **Whether Angelina should be permitted to make up** the educational opportunities she has been denied.

5. **Whether the school should be required to provide compensatory education.**

6. **What systemic changes are needed** to prevent similar violations in the future.

## Relief Requested

I am requesting the following relief:

1. Reversal of all decisions affecting Angelina as set forth in my formal appeal
2. Immediate convening of an IEP Team meeting
3. Reclassification of all 18 absences as medically excused
4. Permission for Angelina to make up missed work, final exams, and credit recovery
5. Compensatory education for the educational opportunities denied
6. Written confirmation of the steps the district will take to ensure compliance

## Procedural Information

I understand that:

- The Fair Hearing must be scheduled within a reasonable timeframe
- I have the right to be represented at the hearing
- I have the right to present evidence and witnesses
- The School Committee must issue a written decision
- I have the right to seek further review of the School Committee's decision

I am available to meet with the School Committee at its earliest convenience. Please contact me to schedule the hearing.

## Deadline

I am requesting that the Fair Hearing be scheduled no later than **30 days from the date of this letter**, in accordance with applicable timelines.

Sincerely,

Sarah Crooker
Parent of Angelina Crooker
