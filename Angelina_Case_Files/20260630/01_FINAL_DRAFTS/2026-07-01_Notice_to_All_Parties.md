## Notice of Procedural Violations

Wareham High School has committed at least 18 procedural violations against Angelina Crooker, including but not limited to:

### Federal Law (IDEA / 34 CFR Part 300)

- 34 CFR § 300.321 — Failure to convene IEP Team
- 34 CFR § 300.322 — Failure to ensure parent participation
- 34 CFR § 300.503 — Failure to provide prior written notice
- 34 CFR § 300.300 — Failure to obtain parental consent
- 34 CFR § 300.504 — Failure to provide procedural safeguards notice
- 34 CFR § 300.501 — Failure to maintain or provide access to records

### Massachusetts State Law

- 603 CMR 28.00 — Failure to follow IEP development and review timelines
- 603 CMR 23.00 — Failure to respond to records request within 10 business days
- MGL c. 71, § 37H 1/2 — Violation of due process requirements
- MGL c. 76, § 1B — Denial of equal access to education
- MGL c. 119, § 21 — Selective enforcement of truancy policy
