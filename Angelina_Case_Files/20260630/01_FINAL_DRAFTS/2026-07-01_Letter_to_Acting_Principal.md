# Letter to the Acting Principal

**Date:** July 1, 2026
**From:** Sarah Crooker, parent
**To:** Acting Principal, Wareham High School
**Re:** Urgent Matters Requiring Immediate Attention — Angelina Crooker

---

Dear Acting Principal,

I am writing to introduce myself and to bring several urgent matters to your attention regarding my daughter, Angelina Crooker, a Grade 9 student at Wareham High School with an active IEP.

I understand that you are serving as Acting Principal during Principal Palladino's absence (June 26 - July 7, 2026). I appreciate your willingness to serve in this capacity and look forward to working with you.

## Urgent Matters

I am bringing the following matters to your attention:

1. **Procedural Violations** — The school has committed at least 18 procedural violations affecting Angelina.

2. **Pending IEP Team Meeting** — I have repeatedly requested an IEP Team meeting, and the school has refused to convene one.

3. **Records Request** — I submitted a records request on June 26, 2026, and have not received a response.

4. **Summer Programming** — Angelina is being denied access to summer credit recovery and summer school programming.

5. **Formal Appeal** — I have filed a formal appeal dated July 1, 2026, requesting reversal of all adverse decisions.

6. **School Committee Fair Hearing** — I have requested a Fair Hearing before the School Committee.

## What I Need From You

I am requesting that you, as Acting Principal:

1. **Acknowledge receipt** of this letter and the other documents I have submitted.

2. **Confirm your authority** to engage with me on these matters, or identify the appropriate person to do so.

3. **Ensure that the records request is processed** by July 8, 2026.

4. **Ensure that the school responds to my formal appeal** by July 15, 2026.

5. **Forward this letter and all related correspondence** to Principal Palladino for his review upon his return.

6. **Schedule a meeting with me** at your earliest convenience to discuss these matters.

## Background Information

For your reference, the key facts are:

- Angelina is a Grade 9 student with an active IEP
- Beginning in late May 2026, she experienced an acute medical condition
- All 18 absences in June 2026 were medically documented and communicated to the school
- The school has denied her the opportunity to take final exams, enroll in summer credit recovery, and participate in summer school
- The school has refused to convene an IEP Team meeting
- The school has not provided prior written notice
- The school has not obtained parental consent

## Documentation

I have provided the school with extensive medical documentation, parent communications, and records. I am prepared to provide additional documentation as needed.

## Conclusion

I am committed to working with the school to resolve these matters in a way that protects Angelina's rights. I trust that you will give these matters your immediate attention.

I am available to meet with you at your earliest convenience. Please contact me to schedule a meeting.

Sincerely,

Sarah Crooker
Parent of Angelina Crooker
