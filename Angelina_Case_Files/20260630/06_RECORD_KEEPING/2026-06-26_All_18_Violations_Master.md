# All 18 Procedural Violations — Master Document

**Prepared:** June 26, 2026
**Student:** Angelina Crooker
**School:** Wareham High School
**Maintained by:** Sarah Crooker (parent)

## Purpose

This document lists each of the 18 documented procedural violations committed by Wareham High School, with citations to the specific legal authority violated.

## The 18 Violations

### IDEA / Federal Violations (34 CFR Part 300)

1. **Failure to Convene IEP Team** — 34 CFR § 300.321, 300.324
   School made multiple decisions affecting Angelina's placement without convening her IEP Team.

2. **Failure to Ensure Parent Participation** — 34 CFR § 300.322
   School did not notify parent of any IEP meetings, did not schedule meetings at mutually agreeable times, and did not offer alternative participation methods.

3. **Failure to Provide Prior Written Notice** — 34 CFR § 300.503
   School excluded Angelina from final exams, credit recovery, and summer programming without providing prior written notice.

4. **Failure to Obtain Parental Consent** — 34 CFR § 300.300
   School made changes to Angelina's educational program without obtaining informed parental consent.

5. **Failure to Review Existing Evaluation Data** — 34 CFR § 300.305
   School did not review whether absences were disability-related before implementing consequences.

6. **Failure to Consider Accommodations** — 34 CFR § 300.320
   School did not consider whether absences warranted accommodations under Angelina's existing IEP.

7. **Failure to Provide Procedural Safeguards Notice** — 34 CFR § 300.504
   School did not provide Sarah Crooker with a copy of the procedural safeguards notice.

8. **Failure to Maintain Appropriate Records** — 34 CFR § 300.501
   School did not maintain or provide access to all records related to Angelina's case.

### Massachusetts State Violations (603 CMR)

9. **Failure to Follow 603 CMR 28.00** — IEP Development Timeline
   School did not develop or review Angelina's IEP within required timelines.

10. **Failure to Follow 603 CMR 23.00** — Records Request Response
    School has not responded to records request within 10 business days.

11. **Failure to Provide Prior Written Notice (State)** — 603 CMR 28.00
    School did not provide state-required prior written notice.

12. **Failure to Convene IEP Team (State)** — 603 CMR 28.00
    School did not convene IEP Team as required by state regulations.

### MGL / Statutory Violations

13. **Violation of MGL c. 71, § 37H 1/2** — Due Process
    School imposed consequences without following due process requirements.

14. **Violation of MGL c. 76, § 1B** — Equal Access
    School denied Angelina access to her educational program based on disability-related absences.

15. **Violation of MGL c. 119, § 21** — Selective Enforcement
    School selectively invoked truancy policy without filing 51A report if it genuinely believed neglect was occurring.

### Additional Procedural Violations

16. **Failure to Acknowledge Medical Documentation** — School ignored 16+ doctor's notes provided for absences.

17. **Failure to Communicate with Parent** — School did not respond to multiple emails, phone calls, and in-person visits.

18. **Failure to Document Decision-Making** — School has no documentation of how or why decisions were made.

## Pattern

These 18 violations are not isolated incidents. They represent a systemic pattern of non-compliance with federal and state special education law. Each violation is independently actionable; together, they constitute a clear denial of FAPE.
