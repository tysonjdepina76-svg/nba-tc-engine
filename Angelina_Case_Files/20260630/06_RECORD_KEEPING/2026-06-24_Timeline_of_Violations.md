# Timeline of Procedural Violations — Angelina Crooker

**Period:** May 2026 — June 2026
**Prepared:** June 24, 2026
**Maintained by:** Sarah Crooker (parent)

## Purpose

This document provides a chronological timeline of the procedural violations committed by Wareham High School in Angelina Crooker's case.

## Timeline

### Late May 2026

- **Week of May 25, 2026:** Angelina begins experiencing acute medical symptoms. Parent contacts school to inform them of medical situation.
- **May 28, 2026:** Parent provides initial doctor's note. School acknowledges receipt but takes no further action.
- **May 29, 2026:** Parent emails school counselor requesting meeting to discuss Angelina's medical situation. No response received.

### Early June 2026

- **June 1, 2026:** Parent calls school to report Angelina will be absent for medical appointment. Doctor's note provided.
- **June 2, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). School does not acknowledge.
- **June 3, 2026:** Parent emails teachers to request homework. One teacher responds. IEP Team not convened.
- **June 4-5, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). School takes no action.
- **June 8, 2026:** Parent requests IEP Team meeting via email. No response.
- **June 9-10, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). School takes no action.

### Mid-June 2026

- **June 11, 2026:** Parent calls school to ask about final exam schedule. School says "she's not allowed to take exams."
- **June 12, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No IEP Team meeting.
- **June 14, 2026:** Parent emails principal requesting emergency meeting. No response.
- **June 15-16, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No action by school.
- **June 17, 2026:** Parent in-person visit to school. Told to "wait until she's back."
- **June 18, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No action.

### Late June 2026

- **June 19, 2026:** Angelina returns to school for one day. Teachers deny her access to make up work. No prior written notice provided.
- **June 22, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No action.
- **June 23, 2026:** Angelina returns to school. Told she is "not on the final exam list." No prior written notice.
- **June 24, 2026:** Parent emails principal requesting immediate IEP Team meeting. No response.
- **June 25, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No action.
- **June 26, 2026:** Parent files records request. No acknowledgment.
- **June 29-30, 2026:** Angelina absent (medically documented). No action.

## Summary of Procedural Failures

The following procedural violations occurred repeatedly throughout this timeline:

1. **No IEP Team meetings convened** — despite multiple parent requests
2. **No prior written notice provided** — for any decision affecting Angelina
3. **No parental consent obtained** — for any change to Angelina's program
4. **No documentation requests acknowledged** — parent communications ignored
5. **No accommodation review** — for disability-related absences
6. **No procedural safeguards provided** — parent not informed of rights
7. **No alternative means of engagement** — school did not offer phone/video meetings
8. **No records request response** — within the 10-day requirement

## Pattern

This timeline demonstrates a clear pattern of systemic non-compliance with IDEA, 603 CMR 28.00, and 34 CFR Part 300. The school's actions are not isolated incidents but rather a continuous pattern of procedural violations.
