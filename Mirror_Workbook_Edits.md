## Edits Applied

### 1. Grounding Tool Clarified
Added/clarified the **5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique** in Module 1 as the primary grounding tool readers can return to when the workbook becomes emotionally overwhelming.

### 2. Module 1 Purpose Clarified
Clarified that **Module 1** is about identifying old labels, inherited stories, and the identity the reader was handed by trauma, systems, family, or the streets.

### 3. Module 7 Purpose Clarified
Clarified that **Module 7** is where the reader begins choosing and building a new identity — the future story they want to live into.

### 4. Duplicate Technical Definitions Reduced
Trimmed repeated clinical definitions inside module glossary areas and pointed readers to the final glossary when a full definition is already provided there.

### 5. Letter Exercises Labeled by Purpose
Kept the letter-writing exercises but clarified their different purposes:

- **Bargaining Letter** — for the part of grief that still believes the loss could have been prevented or traded for.
- **What I Lost Letter** — for naming the full weight of what was lost.
- **Grief Letter** — for ongoing grief maintenance and saying what still needs to be said.
- **Legacy Letter** — for speaking to the younger self, children, or future people the reader wants to reach.

### 6. The 90-Day Rule Preserved
Kept the **90-day rule** in Module 8 and preserved the direct language around not quitting during the first 90 days of new behavior.

## Editorial Decision

The workbook was not rewritten into a cleaner but colder version. The edits were applied surgically. The core voice remains raw, direct, repetitive where repetition is useful, and grounded in lived experience.
