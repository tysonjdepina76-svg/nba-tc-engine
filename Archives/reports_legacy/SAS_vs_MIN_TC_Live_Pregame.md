## TC FORMULA SHEET

```
TC PTS = pts × 0.85
TC REB = reb × 0.12
TC AST = ast × 0.10
TC 3PM = 3pm × 0.08
TC TOT = TC_PTS + TC_REB + TC_AST + TC_3PM

LINE (pts)  = TC_PTS × 0.88 → rounded to nearest whole
EDGE       = TC_TOT − LINE (player contribution)

Q factor   = 0.55 applied to BASE stats when Questionable
OUT factor = 0.0 (zero contribution)
```
