# Query-to-Section Mapping

Where to find answers to common questions in SEC filings.

## Business & Operations

| Question | Filing | Section |
|----------|--------|---------|
| What does the company do? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| What are the main business segments? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| What products/services do they offer? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| Who are their customers? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| What are their competitive advantages? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| How many employees? | 10-K | Item 1. Business |
| What properties do they own? | 10-K | Item 2. Properties |

## Financial Performance

| Question | Filing | Section |
