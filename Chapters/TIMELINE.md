# TIMELINE — Tyson DePina

---

## 1976 — The Beginning

**April 19, 1976** — Born, New Bedford, Massachusetts (Cape Verdean)

---

## 1981 — The First Loss

**February 5, 1981** — Sister Tanisha born

**February 17, 1981** — Tanisha dies of SIDS (12 days old). Tyson was 4 years, 10 months old.

**~Early 1980s** — Grandfather dies

**~Early-mid 1980s** — Great-grandmother dies

---

## 1986-1987 — The Champion

**1986, Age 10** — Competed against Team Canada. 400+ trophies. Billy Blanks USA JR All-Stars. Sports page headline: *"Youngster's quiet nature belies fighting spirit."*

**1987, Age 11** — Competed in Trinidad and Tobago

---

## 1988 — The Second Lesson

**1988, Age 12** — Miguel and Paul G. killed. The deaths that taught Tyson to hit first. Scotty Wat also killed while Tyson was locked up.

---

## 1990 — The Arrest

**September 1990, Age 14** — Arrested. Courthouse cell. Charges: Possession of firearm (2 counts), Possession of Class A with intent to distribute, Assault and Battery (5 counts). Committed to DYS until 18. Brockton Y. Fuller Memorial Hospital (30-day psych eval). Foster home in Bridgewater across from SECC.

---

## 1991 — The Water Tower

**1991, Age 15** — Efrain. Water tower incident.

---

## 1992 — DYS Release

**~1992, Age 16** — Released from DYS

---

## 1994-1996 — The War Years

**~1994, Age 17-18** — Back on parole violation. Drama hunting. South First vs. West End fight. Multiple stabbings. War escalates.

**1996, Age 20** — Scotty Wat killed (while Tyson was locked up)

---

## 2004 — The Raid

**November 2004, Age 28** — Rivet Street raid at Big B's house. SWAT NBPD Bristol County Sheriff DEA. Stun grenades through 3 windows. Empty kilo wrapper, weed, Scarface bullet replica scene. No arrest.

---

## 2005 — The First Federal Case

**November 2005, Age 29** — Federal indictment. 21 arrested. 12.5 kilos cocaine, 100g+ black tar heroin, kilo crack, 16 guns including AK-47. Single heroin count. Facing 30 to life as career offender. Case dropped when government could not prove 100-400g quantity. Released.

---

## 2006-2015 — The Free Years

**2006-2015, Ages 30-39** — Running the streets. Free years.

---

## 2015-2016 — The Sinaloa Years

**2015-2016** — The Sinaloa trip. Shareek. Snack. Beck. El Paso. Juarez. Half kilo runs. Beck gets arrested in Phoenix.

---

## 2016 — The Second Federal Case

**November 2016, Age 40** — Second federal case. Conspiracy 100-400g heroin/fentanyl with cousin Shareek. Arrested.

**2016-2024, Ages 40-48** — Inside. The 8 years. John Stanfa. The Four Wisemen. The decision. The work.

---

## 2024 — Coming Home

**November 2024, Age 48** — Released from federal prison. On probation. Dedicated life to helping youth.

---
