## What "Self-Edge" Actually Is

```
File: Projects/tc_math.py, Line 49-81
Function: over_under_signal(projection, line, test="selfedge")

Logic:
  edge = projection - line
  If edge > threshold → OVER signal
  If edge < -threshold → UNDER signal  
  Otherwise → NO_PLAY

Source of line (market_line): ESPN odds fetcher (espn_odds_fetcher.py)
Source of projection: Per-player season average adjusted for matchup/venue
```

The self-edge compares **what TC projects** vs **what the market says**. When TC is HIGHER than the market, that's your edge to go OVER. When TC is LOWER, you go UNDER.

## The Real Engine: gen_wnba_today.py

```
File: Projects/gen_wnba_today.py
Deps: src/wc_self_edge.py, src/tc_math_encoder.py
Pipeline: ESPN scoreboard → per-game boxscores → season avg → self-edge vs. market line
```

This engine:
1. Pulls the **actual WNBA scoreboard** from ESPN for the date
2. Fetches **per-player boxscores** with season stats
3. Computes **per-player rolling season averages**
4. Applies **matchup adjustments** (home/away, opponent defense)
5. Compares to market lines from ESPN odds
6. Generates **unique projections per player per stat**
