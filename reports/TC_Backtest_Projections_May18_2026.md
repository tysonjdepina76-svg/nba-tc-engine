## Formula

- PTS TC = floor(points × 0.85)
- REB TC = floor(rebounds × 0.80)
- AST TC = floor(assists × 0.75)
- 3PM TC = floor(3pm × 0.80)
- Q = TC × 0.55
- OUT = 0
- Edge = Market Line − TC Target
